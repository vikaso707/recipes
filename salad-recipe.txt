recepie for ceaser salad

1 icebearg lettuce medium
2 slice bread
1 lemon
4 clove garlic
8-10 cherry tomatoes
2T Olive oil
1 medium block cheese
handful of raisins
handful of walnuts (preferebly caramelized)

Put the lemon juice into a dish and add garlic and olive oil into it and mix thoughly add grated cheese to the mix and rasins and walnuts to it. Finally add the lettuce and cherry tomatoes to the mix along with the crutons.
